\section{Introduction}

The last three years produced a remarkable wave of medical foundation models.
Generalist medical AI was articulated as a vision in 2023. Medical LLMs
including Med-PaLM, Med-PaLM 2, Med-PaLM M, and Med-Gemini approached
expert-level performance on closed-domain medical question answering.
Imaging and pathology models such as MedSAM, RETFound, UNI, and
Prov-GigaPath demonstrated transfer across modalities, sites, and labels.

In parallel, general-purpose transformers continued to set new standards.
The original transformer architecture introduced the attention mechanism
that displaced recurrence in sequence modeling. BERT then established the
pretraining-then-finetuning paradigm for language understanding, and
GPT-style decoder-only models showed that scale alone could unlock
emergent capabilities.
