\section{Introduction}

The last three years produced a remarkable wave of medical foundation models.
Generalist medical AI was articulated as a vision in 2023~\cite{moor2023gmai}.
Medical LLMs (Med-PaLM, Med-PaLM 2, Med-PaLM M, Med-Gemini) approached
expert-level performance on closed-domain medical question answering
\cite{singhal2023medpalm,singhal2025medpalm2,tu2023medpalmm,saab2024medgemini}.
Imaging and pathology models (MedSAM, RETFound, UNI, Prov-GigaPath)
demonstrated transfer across modalities, sites, and labels
\cite{ma2024medsam,zhou2023retfound,chen2024uni,xu2024provgigapath}.
