\section{Related Work}

Transformers~\cite{vaswani2017attention} have become the dominant architecture
for both vision and language tasks. Residual networks~\cite{he2016resnet} first
established that very deep networks can be trained reliably by introducing
skip connections. BERT~\cite{devlin2018bert} popularized the pretraining-then-
finetuning paradigm for language understanding. Generative adversarial
networks~\cite{goodfellow2014gan} introduced a fundamentally different training
objective based on a minimax game between generator and discriminator.
