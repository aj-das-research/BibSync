\section{Background}

Transformers~\cite{vaswani2017attention} introduced self-attention as a
replacement for recurrence in sequence modeling. BERT~\cite{devlin2019bert}
extended this to bidirectional pretraining. Recent advances
\cite{ghost_paper_2024} use these ideas in new domains.

In a separate line of work, retrieval-augmented methods
\cite{another_missing_2023} combine parametric and non-parametric knowledge.
The original transformer paper is also discussed in passing
\cite{vaswani2017attention}.
